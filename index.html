About Me

I am a PhD student in Computer Science at FudanNLP, Fudan University, supervised by Xuanjing Huang. I also collaborate closely with Jing Shao and the AI45/OpenTrust team. Before joining Fudan, I received my B.S. degree from the Computer Science Elite Class at Beijing Institute of Technology.

My research focuses on computer-use agents and GUI agents, safety alignment and post-training for LLMs/VLMs, and reinforcement learning for diffusion LLMs. I am interested in scalable agentic RL, GUI-tool orchestration, verifiable sandbox environments, multimodal safety evaluation, and reward modeling for safer and more capable AI systems.
